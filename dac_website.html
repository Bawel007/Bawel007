About dac

Our Story

A Greek word. A Ghanaian mission. A commitment to ethical excellence.

Our Philosophy

Built on revelation.
Driven by results.

De Apokalupsis — from the ancient Greek ἀποκάλυψις — means "revelation," "unveiling," or "disclosure." It speaks to our core belief: that truth, brought to light with precision and professionalism, is the most powerful instrument in resolving financial disputes.

We founded dac on the conviction that debt recovery in Ghana deserved better — more ethical, more transparent, and more effective. Too many businesses were losing money not because the debts weren't recoverable, but because recovery methods were unprofessional, data practices were unsafe, and legal processes were opaque.

dac exists to change that. We combine rigorous commercial recovery with trusted legal partnerships, backed by full Data Protection Commission registration and a commitment to ethical conduct in every engagement.

We are registered with the Registrar General's Department, compliant with Ghana's Data Protection Act (Act 843), and our partner lawyers are in good standing with the General Legal Council. Everything we do is above board, by design.

Enforcement Tools Available

Court Enforcement Methods

Writ of Summons

Formal court process initiating legal proceedings against the debtor.

Summary Judgment

Fast-track judgment for clear-cut cases where the debtor has no genuine defence.

Writ of Fi Fa

Court-authorised seizure and sale of the debtor's assets to satisfy the judgment.

Garnishment

Direct attachment of the debtor's bank accounts to recover funds.

Charging Order

Securing the debt against the debtor's property until the debt is repaid.

Mareva Injunction

Emergency court order to freeze the debtor's assets, preventing dissipation.

Client Consent & Disclosure

Before any legal escalation, dac provides full written disclosure of all fees, lawyer arrangements, and expected timelines. Written client consent is obtained before any legal action is filed. Clients may also choose their own lawyer, though dac recommends its trusted legal partners for seamless case management.
